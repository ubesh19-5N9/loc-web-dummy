About Us

Mission

Our aim is to bring up the culture of programming among the students right from their induction days. We help one another in improving our skills as developers for the professional world.

Vision

We desire to see a community of students bringing up each other in various aspects towards becoming successful developers. We dream of a time when house parties involve code jam sessions, when talking code is fun and the day isn't far.

Our Partners

We as an individual can make a change, but with partners like these we can make a revolution.

HackerEarth

We are proud to have HackerEarth as our Platform and Activity Partner. With their tools we are able to engage our community in a very effective way.
